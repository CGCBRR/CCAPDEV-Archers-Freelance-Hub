Archer's Freelance Hub is a dedicated platform connecting De La Salle University (DLSU) students with freelance opportunities. Our mission is to empower DLSU talents by providing a space where they can showcase their skills, connect with potential clients, and find meaningful freelance work.

Whether you're a student looking to gain experience or a client seeking top-tier talent, Archer's Freelance Hub is your go-to destination for all things freelance within the DLSU community.

Bridging the gap between talent and oppurtunity.

Our goal is to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity, by promoting entrepreneurship, practical experience, and meaningful collaboration exclusively within the university community.


At Archer's Freelance Hub, we have developed a freelance marketplace place that allows a two-sided interaction between sellers (freelancers) and buyers (student clients) which are regulary updated.


Currently, our platform has already provided 50,000+ freelancers a place to publish their works and gain experience. Additionally, our community of 200,000+ active users was able to work together and contribute to the 40,000+ completed projects.
